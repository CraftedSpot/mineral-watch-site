What You'll Be Notified About

New Drill Permits

When an operator files Form 1000 to drill on or near your property. This is your first warning sign.

Drilling Starts (Spud)

When a rig actually moves onto the location (Spud). The clock has officially started.

Well Completions

When the well is finished and oil starts flowing. This signals that royalty checks are coming soon.

Operator Transfers

When operational control changes hands (e.g., Chesapeake to Devon). Critical for updating your payment info.

Common Questions

What counts as a "property"?

A property is a unique section-township-range location (e.g. 28-14N-04W). If you have multiple wells in one section, it counts as 1 property.

What is "API Well Tracking"?

This allows you to track specific wells anywhere in the state by their API number, even if you don't own the land. Great for watching nearby horizontal drilling or tracking wells you're interested in purchasing.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no contracts. You can cancel your subscription instantly from your dashboard and retain access until the end of your billing period.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We never share your property locations or data with third parties. We are a monitoring service, not a mineral buyer.
