Common Questions

What is Mineral Watch?

Mineral Watch is a mineral intelligence platform for Oklahoma. It automatically links your properties, wells, documents, OCC filings, and well records into one organized system. You get daily alerts when anything happens on your sections, side-by-side comparison of pooling options from surrounding orders, AI-powered document extraction, production reports, and revenue modeling — all without manually searching the OCC website.

What counts as a "property"?

A property is a unique section-township-range location (e.g., 28-14N-04W). If you have multiple wells in one section, it counts as 1 property. We also monitor your 8 adjacent sections automatically.

What are document credits?

Document credits let you upload deeds, pooling orders, and division orders for AI extraction. One document = one credit. Monthly credits reset each billing cycle. Annual plans include bonus credits that never expire.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel instantly from your dashboard and keep access until the end of your billing period.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We never share your property locations or documents with third parties. We are a monitoring service, not a mineral buyer or landman operation.

What's the difference between wells and properties?

Properties are monitored by section-township-range. Wells are tracked by their specific 10-digit API number. You can add wells anywhere in Oklahoma, even outside your property sections.

What are bonus document credits?

Annual plans include a one-time bonus of permanent credits (75 to 2,500 depending on plan) that never expire while you're subscribed. These are in addition to your monthly credits.
