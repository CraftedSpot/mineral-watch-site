Common Questions

What counts as a "property"?

A property is a unique section-township-range location where you own mineral rights. If you have interests in the same section from different sources, it still counts as one property.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes! Upgrade instantly to add more properties. Downgrade at any time and the change takes effect at your next billing cycle.

What data sources do you monitor?

We monitor the Oklahoma Corporation Commission (OCC) RBDMS database for well permits, drilling activity, and status changes. Standard plans and above also get pooling order notifications.

How quickly will I get alerts?

We check OCC records daily. When new activity appears on your properties, you'll receive an email alert that same day with clear explanations and direct links to official records.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We never share your property information with third parties, and we never make offers to buy your minerals. Your data is encrypted and stored securely.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your account settings and you'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
