Marketing Automations
Automated workflows leveraging your existing monitoring data
Weekly County Activity Digest
Sends prospects a weekly email showing new OCC filings in their counties of interest. Personalized per prospect's property locations. Uses existing monitoring data.
Frequency: Weekly (Mon 8am) Recipients: 12 prospects Last sent: Feb 10
Feature Announcements
Template-driven feature announcement emails. Fill in feature name, description, benefit → auto-sends to all active users and warm leads.
Trigger: Manual Recipients: All users + leads Last sent: Jan 28
Welcome Email Sequence
3-email onboarding series over 10 days: introduce MW, show how to add first property, highlight alert features. Drives activation.
Trigger: New sign-up Recipients: All new users Not configured
Credit Usage Nudge (Upsell)
When a trial user hits 80% of their free credits, send email showing what they've discovered and what upgrading unlocks. Soft upsell with real account data.
Trigger: 80% credit usage Recipients: Trial users Not configured
Monthly Portfolio Report
Monthly email summarizing activity on monitored properties — permits, completions, operator changes. Demonstrates ongoing value, reduces churn.
Frequency: 1st of month Recipients: Paid users Not configured
Forum Trend Auto-Draft
Auto-generates monthly forum content from OCC trends — active counties, new operators, unusual patterns. Creates draft for your review before posting.
Frequency: Monthly Channel: MR Forum Not configured
"New Well Near You" Prospect Alerts
When a new drilling permit is detected near a prospect's property, send a personalized alert demonstrating MW's value in real-time.
Trigger: New permit match Recipients: Prospects + trial users Not configured
30-Day Upgrade Nudge
For free-tier users active 30+ days, send targeted email showing what they're missing. Include their actual property activity as proof of value.
Trigger: 30 days active on free Recipients: Free tier users Not configured

Competitive Positioning
Mineral Watch's differentiation is not about being "better than Enverus" — it's about being different. Oklahoma-specific, OCC-native, built for individual owners and small firms at a fraction of enterprise pricing. Core message: "Mineral Watch is your Oklahoma watchdog — the simplest way to know what's happening on and around your minerals, before it's too late."
